About Me

I’m a PhD candidate at Université Paris-Saclay under the supervision of Marc Glisse and Pascal Massart. Member of the research teams Datashape, Celeste and Probabilité and Statistique (LMO). I collaborate with the medtech company Metafora through the program Paris PhD region.

Current Work

My research lies at the intersection of statistics and machine learning. I’m interested in the development and the analysis of unsupervised learning algorithms, specifically in clustering and density estimation in moderately high dimension. I’m also interested in application to flow cytometry data.

Research Interests

  • Unsupervised Learning
  • Dimensionality reduction in density estimation
  • Topological data analysis
  • Application of machine learning to biology
